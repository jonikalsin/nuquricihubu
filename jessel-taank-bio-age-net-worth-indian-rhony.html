Jessel Taank Net Worth

Jessel Taank flaunted a net worth of about $1 million in 2022.

This fashion publicist and brand consultant started her career in public relations at Karla Otto New York back in Feb 2008. After working there for almost 4 years, she then jumped ship to Michael Kors and worked there for over 2 years.

In June 2016, she then took the job as the “director of public relations” at Westfield. And 3 ½ years later, the star went on to join BWR Public Relations as its vice president.  But still unsatisfied with her work, Jessel finally founded The Right Now in Oct 2020 and also founded The Know in July 2022.

As for her education, Jessel attended UC Santa Barbara from 2002 to 2003 for her bachelor’s degree in communications and media. Whilst also attended King’s College Lonon from 2001 to 2005 pursuing a bachelor’s in English language and literature.
