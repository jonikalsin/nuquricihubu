Charizard (Japanese: リザードン Lizardon) is a Normal-class Rookie Tretta. It is one of the Promotional Tretta.

Tretta data

Pokémon description
It flies in search of formidable enemies. When it breathes intense flames, the flame on its tail also flares up.

Release information

This Tretta was packaged with the Pokémon Mega Ring Special Set: Mega Charizard Y and the Pokémon Tretta Mega Box, both released on December 12, 2014. Both were among numerous items of merchandise released as part of the celebrations for the relocation of Pokémon Center Tokyo, which moved from its original location in Hamamatsuchō to Ikebukuro, and until January 16, 2015, was named Pokémon Center Mega Tokyo. The Special Set includes a Mega Charizard Y-colored Pokémon Mega Ring, a Moncollé Mega Charizard Y figure, and Charizardite Y, designed to interact with the Mega Ring.

Trivia

On the reverse of the Tretta, the words "Pokémon Center Mega Tokyo" are present where left and right move slots usually are.

Origin

Fire Blast is a move that Charizard can learn in the games via TM38.
