His outstanding play as a starter earned him a spot on the “NFL All-Rookie Team.” From 1981 to 1984, he suffered a number of injuries that prevented him from playing. Separated shoulders, torn knee ligaments, and other injuries were among those sustained.

Despite a couple of bad seasons, he had a fantastic 14-year career with the New York Giants. Throughout his career, he completed 33462 yards in 164 games.
