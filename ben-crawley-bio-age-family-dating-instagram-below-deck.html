Ben Crawley Family

Ben Crawley was born to his mother Connie Crawley. Connie, who we know very little of, seems to have an Asian heritage. On 22 March in 2021, Ben wished his mom a birthday with a post. The post read, “Happy Birthday Mum we all miss you so much beautiful”.

But, losing his mother wasn’t something Ben had imagined happening anytime soon but he did, she died before 2020 and Ben loves to cherish her memory with an IG post. He wrote, “Goodbye beautiful girl. Thank you for showing me what selfless unconditional love really is. You always gave everything you had and I am so grateful. Times may have been hard over the years but the love was raw and real and that’s all that matters. What an honor to have you pass in my arms. And thank you for letting me know you are still here. I love you to eternity and back.”

Meanwhile, the only thing which we know about Ben’s father is he is an artist. His father struggled with Dementia and was 87 years old when he died, his father had him late. His father was an acclaimed artist and one of his works was hung at the Archibald in 1990 whereas one of the others was in Doug Moran in 1992.

He mourns the loss of both of his parents.

Ben Crawley Net Worth

Ben Crawley built an impressive net worth above $250K. Although he is currently working as Foreign Exchange Trader, Ben did pursue a career in the entertainment industry. Ben also teaches Forex & Crypto classes under the name Mindful Trading. He boasts that he focuses and encourages his students to focus on mastering one pattern – the pullback.

Ben himself started trading in 2013. He stated that he moved to Bali while living the dream and lost his house. He learned how to code algorithms and remove emotions from trading decisions and studied trading psychology. For signing up for his course, one can get it for $497, 1 on 1 mentorship for 3 X $769 – All included, and $1997 for 1 on 1 Mentorship – All included.

He seems well-traveled and had done at least one job wherever he went. Back in 2006, he worked in London as Bar Supervisor at Ruby Blue London.

Then, Ben moved to Ibiza, Spain, and landed the job of Hush Bar Ibiza at Bar & Promotions in 2007. He also worked on TV as a presenter for Renegade Films, Movie Network Channels, RGM Artist Management, Australian Broadcasting Corporation, and Ambience Entertainment. He served as brand manager for Red Bull Australia.

Moreover, Ben had done over 5 years’ worth of radio stint with NOVA 91.9 – DMG Radio Australia (Jan 2007 – February 2012).

Ben also worked as Marketing Assistant, Event Specialist, and Resident DJ for Red Bull. He has worked in the props department of the movies Alien: Covenant and Peter Rabbit.

On top of that, he is also a DJ who plays under the name DJ Lazy Susan.

After traveling all over and landing jobs of various nature, Ben started working as a deckhand in 2018 on the docks in the South of France. As Slice reported, a deckhand makes $3,500 to $4,500 a month, and up to $50,000 a season in Below Deck.
