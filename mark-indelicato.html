Mark Indelicato Facts

  • He was 8 years old when he started acting at Philadelphia’s Walnut Street Theater.
  • He was cast as Boq for the “Wicked Day” 5th Anniversary Concert on Broadway in New York City on October 27, 2008, and performed again at the Gershwin Theatre on February 28, 2009, for the “Defying Inequality” benefit.
  • From 2006 to 2010, Mark Indelicato played Justin Suarez, Betty’s nephew, in the ABC comedy-drama series, Ugly Betty, based on Fernando Gaitán’s Colombian telenovela Yo soy Betty, la fea. He co-starred in the show alongside the likes of America Ferrera, Ana Ortiz, Becki Newton, and others.
  • For his role in Ugly Betty, he received several death threats and verbal abuse from people.
  • Mark appeared as Mickey in the 2014 art drama thriller film, White Bird in a Blizzard, based on the novel of the same name by Laura Kasischke. The film stars Shailene Woodley, Eva Green, Christopher Meloni, Shiloh Fernandez, Gabourey Sidibe, Thomas Jane, and Angela Bassett.
  • In 2016, he was seen as Blair Ramos in the supernatural period drama horror television series, Dead of Summer.
  • Mark Indelicato is a gay rights activist has also appeared on a special episode of Dr. Phil about teens who were being bullied after coming out.
