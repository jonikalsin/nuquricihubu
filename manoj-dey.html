Wiki/Biography

Manoj Dey was born on Friday, 12 July 1996 (age 26 years; as of 2022) in Jharia, Jharkhand. His zodiac sign is Cancer. Manoj completed his schooling at Dr SPM College, Sindri, Jharkhand.

Manoj Dey’s childhood picture (extreme left) with his siblings

After completing his higher secondary school education, Manoj wanted to pursue a BTech degree, but his father was not financially strong enough to fund his BTech degree. So, he suggested him to pursue an ITI course; however, his family could not endure the fees of the ITI course; therefore, Manoj decided to sell his computer to pay his fees.
